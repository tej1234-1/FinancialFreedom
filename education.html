Glossary of Financial Terms

Understand commonly used financial terms to build a solid foundation in finance:

  • Budget: A plan for managing income and expenses.
  • Interest: The cost of borrowing money or the return on savings.
  • Asset: Something of value owned by an individual or business.
  • Liability: A financial obligation or debt.
