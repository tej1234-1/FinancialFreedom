Savings Goals

Set specific savings goals to secure your financial future:

  • Emergency Funds: Aim to save 3-6 months of living expenses for unexpected situations.
  • Retirement Savings: Invest in retirement accounts like 401(k) or IRA to build wealth for your golden years.
  • Vacation or Major Purchases: Plan and save for travel, buying a home, or other big-ticket items.

Investment Basics

Understand the fundamentals of investing:

  • Types of Investments: Stocks, bonds, mutual funds, and ETFs are common options.
  • Risk vs. Return: Higher potential returns often come with higher risks. Balance risk based on your goals.

Investment Planning

Create a strategic plan for your investments:

  • Diversified Portfolio: Spread your investments across different assets to reduce risk.
  • Long-term vs. Short-term: Match your investment choices to your time horizon and financial goals.
