50/30/20 Rule

This simple budgeting method divides your income into three categories:

  • 50% Needs: Essential expenses like rent, groceries, and utilities.
  • 30% Wants: Discretionary spending like entertainment and dining out.
  • 20% Savings: Savings, investments, and debt repayment.

Adjust these percentages as needed to suit your financial goals.
