Setting SMART Goals

Learn how to set Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, and Time-bound financial goals:

  • Specific: Define your goal clearly. For example, "Save ₹5,00,000 for a car."
  • Measurable: Track your progress regularly.
  • Achievable: Ensure your goal is realistic given your current financial situation.
  • Relevant: Align your goal with your overall financial plan.
  • Time-bound: Set a deadline to achieve your goal.

Milestones

Break down your financial goals into manageable milestones:

  • Short-term: Save for a gadget or pay off a small debt.
  • Long-term: Plan for a house purchase or higher education.
