Debt Reduction Strategies

Reducing debt requires a clear plan. Here are two popular strategies:

  • Snowball Method: Focus on paying off the smallest debt first, then move to the next smallest. This method builds momentum and motivation.
  • Avalanche Method: Prioritize paying off debts with the highest interest rates first to save more on interest over time.
  • Consolidation: Combine multiple debts into one loan with a lower interest rate for simplified payments.
  • Refinancing: Renegotiate your loan terms to lower interest rates or extend repayment periods.

Credit Score Management

Your credit score plays a crucial role in your financial health. Here are some tips:

  • Factors Affecting Credit Scores: Payment history, credit utilization, length of credit history, credit mix, and new credit inquiries.
  • Improve Your Score:
    • Pay your bills on time.
    • Keep your credit utilization below 30%.
    • Maintain older accounts to increase your credit history length.
    • Limit new credit applications to avoid multiple inquiries.
