Types of Insurance

Understanding the various types of insurance can help you choose the right coverage:

  • Health Insurance: Covers medical expenses and hospitalization costs.
  • Life Insurance: Provides financial support to your dependents in case of your demise.
  • Auto Insurance: Protects against damages or theft of your vehicle.
  • Home Insurance: Covers damages to your property and belongings.
  • Disability Insurance: Replaces a portion of your income if you become unable to work.
